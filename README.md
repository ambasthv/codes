With the IRB project , Volkswagen Financial Services  is preparing  the introduction of the Internal Ratings Based Approach (IRBA)  and managing the entire regulatory application and  approval process with the relevant supervisory authorities .   

A key component is the sub-project Model Development & Methodology  . Here, the IRB models (PD, LGD, CCF) are designed , further developed, documented, and prepared for regulatory approval. The goal is to provide robust, transparent, and regulatorily accepted risk models that can be effectively used in operational lending. 

To strengthen our team, we are looking for a  credit  risk modeler with  sound experience in the development and documentation of IRB models , who  is  responsible for the  technical  design  of the models  and  ensures regulatory  approval  . 

Your tasks: 
In the IRB project, the daily business of an IRB modeler is clearly focused on methodological quality, regulatory compliance, and the approvability of the IRB models. 

 

Technical development, further development and calibration of IRB models (PD, LGD, CCF) taking regulatory requirements into account. 
Analysis of data, model assumptions and risk drivers , and derivation of technically sound model decisions. 
Creation and maintenance of the regulatory-relevant model documentation (e.g. methodology papers, assumptions, limitations, use-test presentation) 
Translation of regulatory requirements (CRR, EBA guidelines, ECB guidelines, BaFin expectations) into concrete model and documentation requirements. 
Close collaboration with validation, governance, data, IT and credit processes , particularly to ensure consistency and use testing. 
Support with reviews, examinations and supervisory meetings , including technical argumentation and answering follow-up questions. 
Identification and processing of findings and measures from validation, audits or supervisory reviews 
 

In short: 


The IRB modeler ensures that IRB models are technically sound, regulatory compliant, transparently documented and approvable – and can be effectively used in the lending business. 

You bring the following:
 

Successfully completed studies in the field of economics, mathematics, physics or a comparable field of study.
Several years of experience in modeling credit risks (PD, LGD, CCF), ideally in an IRBA environment 
good knowledge of the use of SAS (Viya) software
Very good understanding of the regulatory requirements for IRB models (CRR, EBA guidelines, ECB/SSM expectations, BaFin) 
Experience in creating regulatory model documentation and in handling validation and testing. 
Strong analytical skills and a structured, comprehensible way of working. 
Ability to explain complex methodological issues in an understandable way – even to non-technical stakeholders. 
Experience in collaborating in interdisciplinary teams (modeling, validation, processes, governance, IT) 
Excellent German and English skills for working on national and international projects
