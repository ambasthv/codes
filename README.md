
Model Type:	Scorecard for Dual Risk Rating of Corporate and CF: PD Scorecard and LGD for the $75 MM+ revenue segments of Large Corporate and All Cash Flow, Balance Sheet Dependent and Investor Dependent Sponsor and GFB Management Company segments.
Estimation Technique: 	Blended Scorecard: Quantitative and Qualitative (Expert Judgment) 
Model Usage: 	The metrics used in the scorecard will be used to assign PD and LGD ratings for large Corporate clients ($75MM+) and Sponsor Finance clients. The scorecard also covers ratings for Global Fund Banking’s Management Companies.
Downstream Models: 	Credit Decisioning, CECL/IFRS9, Stress Testing, and Capital, Pricing.  Incorporation into these models will be phased in once the dual risk rating, new PDs and LGDs, and new Masterscale have been approved for use and rolled out.
