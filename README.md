As Credit Risk Data Analyst – Credit Administration & Infrastructure, you will be responsible to –

Deploy machine learning type models as executable packages and applications that can automate the calculation and analysis of risk rating (PD/LGD) tasks.

Build tools that will help process data, execute models, and conduct analysis to support the deployment and subsequent uses and monitoring of models; and

Assist in documenting processes, code, and other related artifacts for review by model risk teams and other reviewers.



Skills and Qualification 

Functional Skills:

Strong understanding of predictive models including standard regression models as well as other industry standard machine learning methods.

Understanding of credit risk concepts, credit products, credit portfolio management theory and practice, model governance, and performance measurement.

Strong communication and documentation skills to explain technical concepts to diverse stakeholders.

Curiosity, initiative, and adaptability to work in a developing framework with limited preexisting processes and significant change and transformation.



Technical/Business Skills:

Hands-on experience using Python to develop end-to-end analytical data driven applications and conduct analysis on data including dynamic data driven dashboards.

Good understanding of ML-OPS principles including collaborative coding, version management, CI/CD pipelines and automated testing.

Experience using SQL for data testing, extraction, transformation, statistical analysis, and data visualization.

Experience with Power BI, Alteryx or Tableau workflow automation is helpful.

Familiarity with data visualization tools and reporting methods.

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in a quantitative field shall be an advantage

9-12 years of relevant analytical experience; strong aptitude to learn quantitative modeling concepts quickly.
